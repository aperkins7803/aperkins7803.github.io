Overview

Purpose

Do you have a dog? Do you like to post or look at dogs on social media? Do you not like cats? My plan is to make a social media specifically dedicated to pictures and videos of dogs. Some people don’t like to get into all of the small details of others’ lives, but with my project, they won’t have to. They can look at their feed to have a quick laugh or use it to post and share their dog’s cuteness. They can do this without any intrusion of cats. If this is interesting to you, you should go onto this website and invest.

Audience

My audience is people who love dogs and are willing to spend a good amount of time looking at them or posting about them. This would most likely include teenagers at their parent's home or in college, and dog owners. Because of this, I should include a dog related theme throughout.

Content

Home page

The home page for BowWow will be a feed for social media. On the right, there will be a scrollable feed from whoever you are following. In this feed, there will be the option to comment on posts and comment on comments. You will also be able to see how many comments and likes the post has received. In the top, there will be an option to post a picture, story, or video. On the left side of the page, there will be links to other supposed features of the website including discovering new people and checking out who follows you. The nav will lead to the other pages.

Images for the Home page

Your Page

This page will be very similar to the home page. The difference is that instead of just a posting box on top, you will have a box with a background image, and your profile picture. This will show all of your posts only instead of other people's posts.

Images for the Page 2

Login

THis page will feature a login. Text input will be there for the username and password. There will also be an option lo log in through google and facebook. If a user does not yet have an account, they can press the sign up button at the bottom.
